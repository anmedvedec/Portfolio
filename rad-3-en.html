Business card

Graphic design

One family engaged me in making a business card that they could give their friends from abroad. Besides standard information such as a phone number, they wanted to put their home and cottage address.

Inspired with minimalism, elegant business card present Croatia with blue color and gold print. Instead of telephone icons are initials which address whose telephone number is it. Geometrical shapes mark house destinations on both card sides. With adding Zagreb as capital Croatia city viewer can easier predict the distance of both places.

For this example real data was replaced.
