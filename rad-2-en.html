Poster "Studosh"

Graphic design

Studosh is a info poster for a imaginary film festival created for Design of media systems course mentored by doc. Igor Kuduz.

Poster needed to be in two languages- Croatian and English to navigate and inform visitors of the festival. Also, it has to have a maximum of two colors used on B1 format and contained a map of University North campus in Koprivnica where the festival was located. Stroke was main part of identity. Instead illustrated icons I used typography connected with building from map with program. Upper half of poster contains festival pieces of information on Croatian lanugage and lower half is on English.

Until now, poster was exposed at two exhibitions: Zagreb Design Week and Exhibition of Croatian Design 1718.
