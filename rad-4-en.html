Visual identity "Vafi"

Graphic design

Vafi is international children and youth animation film festival which maintains in Varaždin ever year. Poster was created as a coursce task from Graphic Design. From more than 60 student works mine was chosen as the best.

Since the festival is intended for children and young teens, the Chinese dragon seemed as a fine choice due to its characteristic looks and recognizability. While creating the posters and other materials, I used the previously established VAFI color palette, as well as existing typefaces. I have also noticed that previous VAFI designs heavily utilized geometric shapes, so I created the dragon out of rectangles, triangles and circles. I designed: poster, citylight, bookmarks, catalog cover, t-shirt and animated an video.
