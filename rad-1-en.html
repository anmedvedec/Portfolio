Book "Thanks on a bill"

Graphic design

The book is an author project of analog conversion into a digital media which was created on Postdigital publishing course mentoring by doc.Niko Mihaljević.

Not everyone takes time to analyzes bill after shopping. Most of the bills in Croatia have at the bottom a personalized message to its customers. That message not only presents the way of communication, but also a relationships between two sides. The task turned into monthly collecting bills, which were eventually selected and published as a book. The book faces the reader with a message from the account whose trader reveals on the last pages. The importance of ignored components of bill and awareness of treating customers are two hightlights which present a book.
